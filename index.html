Meet Bob Burr

I'm not a guru with all the answers. I'm a grandpa who's made plenty of mistakes and learned from every one of them.

After serving my country, losing everything, and rebuilding from scratch multiple times, I've learned that the path forward isn't about perfection—it's about persistence, purpose, and the wisdom that comes from walking through fire.

Now I share what I've learned with those who need it most: veterans transitioning to civilian life, formerly incarcerated individuals rebuilding their futures, and at-risk youth looking for a way forward.

How Pain Becomes Purpose

My journey from service to setback to significance

Military Service

Served my country with honor, learning discipline, duty, and the meaning of sacrifice.

The 1990s - Lost Everything

Transition to civilian life brought challenges I wasn't prepared for. Lost my business, my marriage, everything I thought defined me.

After 9/11 - Finding Purpose

Watched the towers fall and knew I had to serve again, but differently. Started mentoring veterans and at-risk youth.

The Ultimate Choice

Faced my darkest moment and chose life. That choice changed everything and showed me my true purpose.

2003-2017 - Rebuilding

Rebuilt my life from scratch, learning that setbacks are setups for comebacks. Started sharing my story.

2017-2021 - Digital Grandpa Born

Launched Digital Grandpa to reach more people with the wisdom I wish I'd had during my darkest days.

2022-2025 - Expanding Impact

Joined forces with Beacon Momentum to scale the impact and help thousands find their way forward.

2025-Future - Your Turn

Now it's your turn to write your comeback story. I'm here to walk alongside you.
